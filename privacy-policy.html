Privacy Policy for WaveApp

Last updated: December 11, 2025

Introduction

WaveApp ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WaveApp mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • API Key: You provide your Wavespeed.ai API key to authenticate with the AI generation service. This key is stored securely on your device using Android's encrypted storage system.
  • Generation Prompts: Text prompts you enter to generate images and videos are sent to Wavespeed.ai servers for processing.
  • Uploaded Media: Images or videos you upload as reference materials are sent to Wavespeed.ai for processing.

Information Automatically Collected

  • Generation History: Records of your generations (prompts, model used, timestamps, results) are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage information to improve functionality.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash and error reports to help us fix bugs.

Advertising Data

  • AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect information about your device and ad interactions. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

How We Use Your Information

  • API Authentication: Your API key is used solely to authenticate requests to Wavespeed.ai.
  • Service Provision: Your prompts and uploaded media are processed by Wavespeed.ai to generate images and videos.
  • App Functionality: Generation history is stored locally to provide you with access to your past creations.
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google AdMob.
  • App Improvement: Anonymous usage data helps us improve the app experience.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your API key and generation history are stored locally on your device using Android's encrypted SharedPreferences.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your data on our servers.
  • Third-Party Services: Data sent to Wavespeed.ai is subject to their privacy policy and terms of service.

Third-Party Services

Wavespeed.ai

  • Purpose: AI image and video generation service
  • Data Shared: API key, prompts, uploaded media

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Advertising
  • Data Collected: Device information, ad interaction data
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Data Retention

  • API Key: Stored on your device until you remove it or uninstall the app.
  • Generation History: Stored locally on your device. You can delete individual entries or clear all history from within the app.
  • Generated Content: Results are stored as URLs. The actual media files are hosted by Wavespeed.ai.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored locally on your device through the app interface.
  • Delete: Remove your API key, clear generation history, or uninstall the app to delete all local data.
  • Opt-Out: Limit ad personalization through your device's ad settings.
